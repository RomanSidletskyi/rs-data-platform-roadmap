# Daily And Weekly Routine

This routine is designed for developers who want language improvement tied to real technical work.

## Daily Routine

### 15-20 Minutes: Reading

- read one technical page, article, issue, or documentation section in English
- note only the phrases that repeat or feel high-value
- ask AI to explain only the difficult parts

### 10 Minutes: Vocabulary Review

- review phrases in Anki or your spaced-repetition tool
- prefer fewer cards reviewed consistently over large unread decks

### 10-15 Minutes: Speaking

- explain one technical concept aloud in English
- use voice AI or record yourself
- focus on clarity before accent perfection

### 10 Minutes: Writing

- rewrite one short engineering artifact in English
- examples: issue summary, commit explanation, architecture paragraph

## Weekly Routine

### Once Per Week: Listening Session

- watch a technical talk with transcript support
- extract useful phrases
- do short shadowing practice

### Once Per Week: Long Speaking Session

- describe one project, module, or system design in English for 5-10 minutes
- ask AI for follow-up questions

### Once Per Week: Writing Review

- take one longer text you wrote
- improve it for structure, precision, and tone
- compare old and new versions carefully

## Rule

Consistency beats intensity.

Twenty useful minutes daily is stronger than one exhausting session you cannot sustain.
