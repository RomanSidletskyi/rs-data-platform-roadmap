# Quizlet

Quizlet is useful for lighter-weight review and quick study sets when you want less overhead than a full long-term retention system.

## What It Is

Use it for fast review sets, especially when you want to rehearse a limited group of terms or phrases.

## Best For

- quick study sets
- short review cycles

## Not Good For

- serious long-term retention by itself
- selecting the right material automatically

## Strengths

- easy to start
- low setup cost

## Weaknesses

- shallower retention than more disciplined spaced-repetition workflows

## Main Risks

- mistaking easy review for durable retention

## Primary Modes

- learning

## Rule

Fast review is helpful, but retention still depends on repeated useful exposure.
