# ADR Exercise

## Goal

Practice writing a short architecture decision record with real trade-offs.

## Scenario

You need to choose between two storage or table-layer options for a Spark-first data platform.

## Tasks

1. Write the problem statement.
2. List constraints.
3. Compare at least two options.
4. Write the chosen decision and trade-offs.
5. Ask AI to point out missing assumptions.
6. Revise the ADR so the decision is defensible.

## Deliverable

- short ADR draft
- AI critique
- revised ADR

## Self-Review Rubric

Score your final version from 1 to 5:

- decision framing: is the problem statement concrete and scoped
- constraints quality: are real technical constraints stated clearly
- option quality: are alternatives compared fairly rather than dismissed lazily
- trade-off visibility: are costs and consequences explicit
- defensibility: can a reviewer understand why this option was chosen now

## Strong Result Looks Like

- the decision is traceable to constraints
- rejected options are still described honestly
- trade-offs are specific, not generic
- the final choice is easy to challenge and defend
