# ELSA Speak

ELSA-style pronunciation tools are useful when you want direct feedback on articulation and speaking accuracy.

## What It Is

Use this class of tool when pronunciation is the bottleneck, especially for technical terms you need to say clearly.

## Best For

- pronunciation correction
- repeated speaking drills
- building confidence with difficult sounds and technical terms

## Not Good For

- broad conversation practice
- architecture reasoning
- long-form writing skill

## Strengths

- focused pronunciation feedback
- useful for repeated targeted drills

## Weaknesses

- limited as a full speaking system
- can stay detached from real communication tasks

## Main Risks

- doing drills with no transfer into real explanations

## How To Use It Well

- practice technical terms directly
- follow pronunciation drills with a real spoken system explanation

## How Not To Use It

- do not let drills replace free speaking practice

## Primary Modes

- speaking

## Rule

Pronunciation drills help most when they support real speaking, not when they replace it.
