# Mochi

Mochi is useful as a lighter spaced-repetition system when you want lower friction than a heavier flashcard workflow.

## What It Is

Use it when you want recurring review without the maintenance cost of a more complex deck system.

## Best For

- lighter-weight spaced repetition
- review of useful phrases and terms

## Not Good For

- deciding what is worth remembering
- replacing deliberate card selection

## Strengths

- lower friction than heavier systems
- easier to keep small and active

## Weaknesses

- still fails if the card quality is weak

## Main Risks

- collecting easy cards that never transfer into real communication

## How To Use It Well

- keep only high-value phrases and short examples

## Primary Modes

- learning

## Rule

Low-friction review is useful only if the reviewed material matters.
