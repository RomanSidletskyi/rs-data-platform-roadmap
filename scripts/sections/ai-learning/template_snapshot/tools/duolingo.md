# Duolingo

Duolingo-style apps are useful for habit formation and low-friction language contact, but weak as a full system for developer English.

## What It Is

Use this class of tool as a habit starter, not as the center of a technical language-learning system.

## Best For

- keeping language contact alive
- beginner momentum

## Not Good For

- technical English for developers
- architecture speaking practice
- professional writing quality

## Strengths

- easy consistency
- beginner-friendly

## Weaknesses

- weak transfer into real developer communication
- limited support for technical reading and writing

## Main Risks

- confusing app streaks with real professional language growth

## Primary Modes

- learning

## Rule

Use habit apps as a supplement, not as your core developer-English workflow.
