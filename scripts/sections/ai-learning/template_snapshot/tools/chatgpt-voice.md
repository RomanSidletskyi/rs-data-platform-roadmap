# ChatGPT Voice

ChatGPT Voice is useful for low-friction speaking practice and spoken explanation of technical topics.

## What It Is

Use it when you need to talk through ideas aloud, practice design-review explanations, or rehearse technical English with fast feedback.

## Best For

- free speaking practice
- technical explanation rehearsal
- interview-style speaking drills

## Not Good For

- detailed pronunciation correction
- exact technical validation by itself

## Strengths

- very low friction
- easy to practice spontaneously on real topics

## Weaknesses

- less precise than dedicated pronunciation tools
- feedback quality depends on how specific your task is

## Main Risks

- speaking loosely without improving structure or clarity

## How To Use It Well

- explain real projects, bugs, and design choices
- ask for follow-up questions
- ask for correction only on major clarity issues

## How Not To Use It

- do not use it as a substitute for deliberate pronunciation practice when that is the bottleneck

## Primary Modes

- speaking
- learning

## Rule

Voice AI is strongest when it rehearses real technical communication, not generic conversation.
