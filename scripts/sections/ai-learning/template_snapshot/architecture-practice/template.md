## Goal

Use one architecture case to practice option comparison, trade-off reasoning, and explicit decision capture.

## Tasks

1. Restate the scenario in your own words.
2. List the real architectural tension.
3. Compare at least two real options.
4. Choose one option and defend it.
5. Write down the main rejected-option reason.
6. Capture one decision-journal entry and one mistake to log.
